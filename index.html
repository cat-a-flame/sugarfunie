About

Hi, my name is Stefánia, I'm working as a User Experience Engineer based in Hungary.

I've always been interested in drawing since I was a child, but I never fully committed to this habit, don't know why.
In 2021, I got fed up with dreaming about becoming an artist, so I bought an iPad and started learning to draw on my own. I keep documenting my journey, to see how much I improve, and to give some inspiration to others ❤️
